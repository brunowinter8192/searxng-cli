## Selection Criteria

General mode: primary sources (foundational papers), authoritative overviews (Lil'Log), comprehensive surveys. Quality over format — the best foundational papers and surveys rank above niche application papers (molecular, speech, video, medical imaging domain-specific papers) and off-topic entries.
