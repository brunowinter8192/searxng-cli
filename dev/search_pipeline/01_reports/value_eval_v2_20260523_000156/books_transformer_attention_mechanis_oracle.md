# Oracle — books × transformer attention mechanism

Top-10 selected from 62 URLs in the oracle pool.

## Selection Criteria

Pool expanded from ~6 to 62 URLs after URL-filter drop — most are research papers applying transformers to niche domains (bearing fault diagnosis, polyp segmentation, drug interaction prediction, satellite imagery) or Stack Overflow Q&A, not books. Priority: explicit Springer/publisher book chapters, book catalog pages, online textbooks (D2L), then comprehensive surveys with book-chapter depth. Off-topic OpenLibrary entries (Cartesian Meditations, History of the World's Fair) are excluded. Narrow domain-application papers rank below foundational references.
